Viniasa Yoga

Vinyasa is a style of yoga characterized by bringing the positions closer together, so that you move imperceptibly from one to the other, using breathing. It is often called "flowing" yoga, sometimes mixed with "powerful yoga".

Hata Yoga

Hata yoga is the path of body control through body positions (asanas) and breathing techniques (pranayama), and it is considered to be a preparation for raja yoga. This type of yoga involves practicing external and internal purification, and that requires self-discipline.

Dates

Restorative Yoga

Restorative yoga is an exercise that slows down and opens your body with passive stretching. If you go to restorative classes, you will hardly move at all, doing only a few positions for an hour.
